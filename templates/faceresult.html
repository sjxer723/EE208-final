About This Web

In this project, we have crawled over ten thousands pages and six thousands photos and faces. Then we created index and used Flask to build a web framework. And we used bootstrap framework to beautify the front page. We have achieved searching by keywords,images or faces. And the results can be sorted by relevance or date. And the result photo will be painted on the page by an ai-drawer. Besides, we use clustering methods k-means to analyze the news.
